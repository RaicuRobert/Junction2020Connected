Share Your Story

The backbone of this project are people who have won the battle against addiction and are willing to discuss about it.
Do you wish to share your experience?

The Process

Step 1

Send us your story

Start by filling in your details at the bottom of the page. Our professionals will read your story and if we can include it in our platform you will be contacted to continue the process.

Step 2

Editing your story

If your story is selected, you will be in direct contact with one of our editors that will help you structure it in a friendly way for our users.

Step 3

Volunteering - Interview

If you want to also talk with people about your story and help them understand you need to pass a screening with one of our professionals.

Step 4

Volunteering - Training

Once you pass the screening you are invited to take part in a few special training sessions that are ment to prepaer you for your live sessions.
